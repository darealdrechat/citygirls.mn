Training Options

City Girls Skills Training offers a variety of training options designed suit both your training objectives, and your budget. We are more than happy to customize or amend any training package, per request.

  • Session Format: Each session consists of tailored drills designed to focus on the four essential growth areas: ball handling, sharp shooting, defensive tactics, and fundamental footwork, as well as confidence and an increased understanding of the game. Whether you are a beginner, intermeditate, or an experienced player, each of our coaches will provide personalized guidance and insight that aims to challenge and inspire competition.
