Our Team

Erin Bolden

Coach

Erin graduated from Augsburg University in 2017 with a BA in Marketing. She currently works at Wells Fargo in Personal Loans and Credit. She is also an assistant girls basketball coach for Minneapolis Washburn High School. While at Augsburg, she played four years of collegiate ball. She really enjoys teaching the game to youth. It is a passion of hers to serve as a role model for their lives on and off the court.

Idalis Riley

Founder/Coach

Idalis graduated from William Penn University in 2018 with a BA in Psychology and Human Services. She currently works for Search Institute as a Project Administrative Assistant where she identifies pathways and assets for youth to succeed. After playing her first two years of collegiate ball at the NJCAA DI level at Danville Area Community College, she transferred to William Penn Univeristy to complete her collegiate basketball career. Having graduated as a scholar student athlete playing in a competitive NAIA DI Heart of America Conference, she built a program focused on developing young athletes to prepare for the next level.

Andre Chatfield

Improvement Manager

Andre graduated from Harvard University in 2018 with a BA in Economics. He currently works at Accenture as a Technology Analyst in the Client and Market sector. While at Harvard, he played four years of collegiate ball and served as a co-captain for their Men's Basketball team his senior year. A 2x Ivy League Champion and NCAA Tournament contender, he enjoys sharing his wisdom and expertise amongst youth aspiring to play at the next level.

Training Options

City Girls Skills Training offers a variety of training options designed suit both your training objectives, and your budget. We are more than happy to customize or amend any training package, per request.

  • Session Format: Each session consists of tailored drills designed to focus on the four essential growth areas: ball handling, sharp shooting, defensive tactics, and fundamental footwork, as well as confidence and an increased understanding of the game. Whether you are a beginner, intermeditate, or an experienced player, each of our coaches will provide personalized guidance and insight that aims to challenge and inspire competition.
