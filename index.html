What we offer

Ball Handling

Dribbling will become second nature. Our drills help build confidence and composure handling the rock.

Sharp Shooting

Can you knock down tough shots? With our shooting formula and repetition, we will build your muscle memory to increase your shooting percentage.

Defensive Tactics

Offense wins games, defense wins championships. From full court defensive slides, box out drills and 2 on 3 competition, our team aims to build a Mamba Mentality.

Footwork & Conditioning

The focus of these off-court workouts is to enhance your balance, build your core and increase your endurance. Our goal is to develop a healthy lifestyle that also applies outside of basketball training.

Our Story

Idalis Riley

Founder/Coach

Idalis grew up playing basketball in Minneapolis, MN. After she graduated high school, she pursued her collegiate basketball career at Danville Area Community College and William Penn University. Returning home in 2018 she picked up coaching, and fell in love with teaching the game, and healthy workout habits. City Serves, previously known as City Girls Basketball Training, was initially a program focused on developing young athletes to prepare for the next level. But why not provide thematic knowledge? City Serves not only focuses on basketball development (though we thrive in this), but we also focus on healthy workout habits that can be conditioned into any lifestyle. Idalis partners with training facilities and youth programs throughout the city, and she also collaborates with former collegiate athletes for group training sessions
