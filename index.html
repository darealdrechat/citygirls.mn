What we offer

Baskettball Fundamental Training

City Serves focuses on 4 units of basketball development: footwork and defense, ball handling, shooting, conditioning and combo drills.

Fitness Training

These off-court workouts are aimed to build your core and increase your endurance. The sessions consist of HIIT, sculpting and agility workouts. The goal is to embed maintainable routines into your daily life.

Our Story

Idalis Riley

Founder/Coach

Idalis grew up playing basketball in Minneapolis, MN. She graduated from Washburn high school, and after pursued her collegiate basketball career. Idalis received a scholarship to play at Danville Area Community College for two years, and also William Penn University to receive her Bachelors in Psychology and Human Resources. Returning home in 2018 she picked up coaching at her old high school and fell in love with teaching the game.
